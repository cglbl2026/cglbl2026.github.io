Beauty of Logic

Special session in honour of Petr Hájek

Professor Petr Hájek passed away in 2016. This year's Czech Gathering of Logicians is combined with Beauty of Logic, a conference held on special occasions and focussing on his research topics. The combined conference therefore welcomes especially contributions developing such topics (set theory, arithmetic, mathematical fuzzy logic, logic in data analysis). Please mark your submissions "Beauty of Logic" if you are submitting to the Beauty of Logic part of the combined conference.
