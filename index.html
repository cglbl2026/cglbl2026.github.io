Beauty of Logic

Special session in honour of Petr Hájek

Professor Petr Hájek passed away in 2016. This year's Gathering of Logicians is combined with Beauty of Logic, a conference held on separate occasions and focussing on his legacy. The combined conference therefore welcomes especially contributions developing the topics studied by Petr Hájek (set theory, arithmetic, mathematical fuzzy logic, logic in data mining). Please mark your submissions "Beauty of Logic" if you are submitting to the Beauty of Logic section of the combined conference.
